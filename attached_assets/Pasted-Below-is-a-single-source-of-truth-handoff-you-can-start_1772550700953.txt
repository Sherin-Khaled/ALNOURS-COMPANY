* Padding X: Desktop 24, Medium 20, Mobile 16
* Nav link size: Desktop 15 (w500), Medium 14, Mobile menu 16
* Gap between links: Desktop 24, Medium 20
* Hover: pill bg Neutral/50
* Active: underline 2px Primary/700 + text Primary/800 *(or pill active, pick one)*

* Primary: height 44–48, radius 14, bg Primary/700 (hover 800)
